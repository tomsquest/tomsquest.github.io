Fynodoro, the Pomodoro Widget

Fynodoro is a Pomodoro timer designed to stay on your screen all the time. It takes up a tiny bit of screen space and can be set to nearly transparent.

I use it to monitor the time dedicated to a specific task ("Let's put a Pomodoro on that part of the code"), or during pair-programming sessions to switch the keyboard every 25 minutes. A notification at the end keeps me on track.
